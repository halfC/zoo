英英词典
音标 /test; tɛst/
  1. n. 检验；试验
  2. vt. 测试；试验
  3. vi. 测试；试验
时 态: tested, testing, tests
名 词: testability
形容词: testable
近义词：
[n.] mental measurement, attempt, effort, endeavor, endeavour, try, experiment
[v.] evaluate, pass judgment, judge, check, examine, be, score
test n.
  1. any standardized procedure for measuring sensitivity or memory or intelligence or aptitude or personality etc
    • the test was standardized on a large sample of students

    synonymous：mental test, mental testing, psychometric test
  2. the act of testing something

    3. synonymous：trial, run
  3. the act of undergoing testing
    • he survived the great test of battle

    synonymous：trial
  4. trying something to find out about it

    5. synonymous：trial, trial run, tryout
  5. a set of questions or exercises evaluating skill or knowledge
    • when the test was stolen the professor had to make a new set of questions

    synonymous：examination, exam
  6. a hard outer covering as of some amoebas and sea urchins
test v.
  1. put to the test, as for its quality, or give experimental use to

    2. synonymous：prove, try, try out, examine, essay
  2. test or examine for the presence of disease or infection

    3. synonymous：screen
  3. examine someone's knowledge of something
    • The teacher tests us every week

    synonymous：quiz
  4. show a certain characteristic when tested
    • He tested positive for HIV
  5. achieve a certain score or rating on a test
    • She tested high on the LSAT and was admitted to all the good law schools
  6. determine the presence or properties of (a substance)
  7. undergo a test
    • She doesn't test well
